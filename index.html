Thesis

From whispered ideas to world-changing ventures.

Historically, “gossip” didn’t mean rumor.
It meant a sacred circle of women and allies - godparents, kin, caretakers - gathering through birth, grief, celebration, survival.

Over time, patriarchy twisted it into something frivolous.

We’re reclaiming the word.

Gossip Group is an invite-only global network of visionary investors, founders, and operators backing the hottest gossip of tomorrow - groundbreaking companies making the future healthier, happier, and more human (H³).

Our thesis is simple: capital should serve community.

And the future of investing?
It doesn’t look like Wall Street.
It looks like a roundtable — with sharp minds, open hearts, and deep conviction.

We don’t just run investment meetings.
We design community experiences.

Trust before transactions.
Imagination meets capital.

Hot gossip for trusted ears only…

Smart money. Sharp minds. Great gossip.
